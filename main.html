Maldives

The Maldives is a tropical paradise located in the Indian Ocean, known for its crystal-clear waters, sandy beaches, and luxury resorts.

  1. Snorkeling and Diving: The Maldives is home to some of the world's best coral reefs, making it a popular spot for snorkeling and diving.
  2. Island Hopping: With over 1,000 islands to choose from, island hopping is a great way to explore the diverse beauty of the Maldives.
  3. Underwater Restaurant: The Maldives is home to the world's first underwater restaurant, offering a unique dining experience.
  4. Spa and Wellness: Many of the luxury resorts in the Maldives offer world-class spa and wellness services to help you relax and unwind.
  5. Water Sports: From surfing to kayaking, the Maldives offers a wide range of water sports activities for adrenaline junkies.
  • Garudhiya: A traditional fish broth served with rice, lime, chili, and onions.
  • Mas Huni: Grated tuna mixed with coconut, onion, and chili, usually eaten for breakfast with flatbread.
  • Bis Keemiya: Fried pastry filled with spiced fish or vegetables, usually served as a snack or appetizer.
  • Fihunu Mas: Grilled fish marinated in chili, ginger, and garlic, a popular dish for seafood lovers.
  • Boshi Mashuni: A salad made with cucumber, onion, and tomato, dressed with lemon and chili, usually served as a side dish.
